About Us

Empowering Broadcast Workflows with Technology, Expertise, and Support

At LynTech, we specialize in delivering cutting-edge Broadcast IT solutions with a focus on Media Asset Management (MAM) systems. With deep expertise in both broadcast engineering and IT infrastructure, we help broadcasters, studios, and media houses streamline their operations from content creation to archiving.

With years of hands-on experience, we provide end-to-end services including system design, installation, configuration, user training, and ongoing technical support. Our solutions are tailored to the unique needs of each client—whether it's setting up a new MAM system, integrating existing workflows, or upgrading legacy infrastructure.

Our team consists of certified professionals with backgrounds in media technology, IT systems, and broadcast operations. We work closely with industry-leading vendors and platforms to ensure our clients benefit from the latest innovations in the broadcast domain.

What We Offer:

  • Broadcast IT Solutions: Design and deployment of scalable, secure, and reliable broadcast IT infrastructure.
  • MAM Installation & Configuration: From small post-production setups to large-scale enterprise installations.
  • Training: Customized training for operators, engineers, and admin staff to ensure smooth adoption and usage.
  • Support Services: Dedicated support for troubleshooting, maintenance, and system upgrades.

At our core, we are problem-solvers who thrive on helping clients optimize their workflows, reduce downtime, and future-proof their media operations.

Let’s build the future of broadcasting together.
